Here's a weird song I made sampling a friend playing a jaw harp and making weird noises. The jaw harp sounds are heavily manipulated. Every tonal aspect of the song, with the exception of the obvious vocal samples, were made from typical jaw harp sounds.
The high hat sound is also from the jaw harp bouncing off her teeth... Kind of painful to think about.


![album-cover-photo](/assets/tv-scope.jpg) 


The music video was made by feeding isolated tracks from the song into a TV modded to behave similar to an oscilliscope. Easy album artwork too!
